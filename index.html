Get To Know Me

About Me

Hi, I'm Vittorio! I'm a Computer Science student at the University of Michigan, where I spend most of my time building things and then figuring out how to make them better.

What I love about coding is that it turns ideas into real systems people can actually use. I'm especially interested in full-stack development, AI, and machine learning, particularly when it comes to optimizing backend systems.

Right now, I'm working at AgentMail, a YC-backed startup, where I help build user-facing demos that clearly explain what's happening behind the scenes while heavy backend processes are running.

I moved from Italy to Miami, Florida when I was 10, and the U.S. gave me access to a world of technology and opportunity that I didn't have before. Since then, I've been obsessed with building and learning through code.

If I'm not coding, I'm probably watching soccer or out and about with friends, but I'm always happy to talk about tech or ideas that sound a little ridiculous but might actually work.
